Features

Replacement Scoring

Every vehicle gets a 0–100 score across 7 factors: age, mileage, cost-per-km, repair history, downtime, brand reliability, and fuel risk.

📸

Invoice Scanning

Photograph any mechanic invoice — handwritten or printed. AI extracts cost, date, category, and line items automatically. Works in English and Spanish.

Smart Alerts

Get emailed when a vehicle exceeds your repair thresholds. Configurable rules: high single-repair cost, repeated breakdowns, recurring issues.

PDF Reports

Three report types: Fleet Summary, Critical Vehicles, and Full Breakdown with per-vehicle factor analysis. Ready for your finance team.

Multi-Currency

19 currencies supported. Invoice amounts auto-detected and converted to your account currency. Spanish and Portuguese number formats handled natively.

Team Access

Invite fleet managers and viewers with role-based permissions. Admins, managers, and read-only viewers. All data scoped to your account.
